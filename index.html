My Favourite Hobby

Reading Books



I love reading books, my favourite books are action books, especially the famous five series. In the series, each books has 4 childeren and one dog but the children love to dog and count him as one them. In each book the five end up falling into adventure. The series is written Enid Blyton and she has told the story in a beautiful way, the story is set in the 1940s-1950s
